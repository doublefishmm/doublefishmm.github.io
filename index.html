HCI Project Description

Health monitoring and Online health guidance

Brainstorm

I am responsible for the mind mapping in Phase I.

We explored ways to improve health in terms of both physical health and mental health. Since mental health is more difficult to detect and adjust, we focus on physical health.

According to the requirement analysis, the functions to be implemented are divided into two parts, Health Check and Online health query.

Health Check:

  • Collect and record users’ health indicators
  • Remind users of some physical problems
  • Remind users to take medication on time
  • Record users’ daily exercise data

Online health query:

  • Online AI consultation
  • Professional doctor consultation
  • Online drug purchase
